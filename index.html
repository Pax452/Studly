About Eleanor

I'm currently reading Mathematics at Oxford (2nd year) and love breaking down problems that feel impossible at first glance. I got A* in both Maths and Further Maths at A Level with AQA, and I know the spec inside out.

I'm especially strong on pure content — proof, series, complex numbers, differential equations — as well as mechanics. I tailor every session to exactly what you need. No prep required. Single sessions are absolutely fine, no commitment required.

Session Preview
Example — Integration by Parts
Eleanor starts with a quick diagnostic question to locate the exact gap. She works through the method step by step using your exam board's style, explains why each step works (not just how), and finishes with a past-paper question for you to try independently while she watches.
