You must not use Studly to harass, abuse, or harm any other user. You must not attempt to circumvent the platform's payment system. You must not share another user's personal information without their consent. Violations may result in immediate account suspension and legal action where appropriate.

Promotional subscriptions (Basic, Featured, Premium) are billed monthly via Stripe and can be cancelled at any time. No refunds are issued for partial months.

1. Who We Are
Studly (studly.it.com) is operated as a sole trader business in the United Kingdom. We are the data controller for personal data collected on this platform. Contact: studly.it@gmail.com.
2. What Data We Collect
Account data: Your name, email address, and region when you sign up.
Profile data: For tutors — subjects, qualifications, bio, photo, availability, and hourly rate. This is publicly visible.
Booking data: Session type, date, time, and notes when you book a session.
Messages: The content of messages sent between users on the platform.
Payment data: We do not store card details. Payments are handled by Stripe, who process and store payment information under their own privacy policy.
Usage data: Basic analytics such as pages visited and features used, collected via essential cookies.
3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to operate the platform, process bookings and payments, enable messaging between users, send verification and notification emails, and improve the service.
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.
4. Legal Basis (UK GDPR)
We process your data on the basis of contractual necessity (to provide the service you signed up for) and legitimate interests (to operate and improve the platform). Where we rely on consent (e.g. cookies), you may withdraw it at any time.
5. Third Parties
Supabase — database and authentication hosting. Data stored on servers within the EU. supabase.com/privacy
Stripe — payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
Vercel — website hosting. vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

6. Cookies
We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can accept or reject non-essential cookies via the banner shown on your first visit.
7. Your Rights
Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also request a copy of your data or object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, email studly.it@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
8. Data Retention
We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance.
